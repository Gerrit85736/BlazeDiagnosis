# Domain Glossary

## Workshop

The mechanical workshop using the Blaze Diagnostics system.

## Tenant

A business account in the system. In a SaaS context, each workshop or organisation may be treated as a tenant.

## Branch

A physical workshop location belonging to a tenant.

## Customer

The person or company whose vehicle is being serviced or repaired.

## Vehicle

The customer's vehicle linked to a job card.

## Job Card

The record of work being performed on a vehicle. It contains the customer complaint, vehicle details, mechanic assignment, quote information, parts tracking, progress notes, and status history.

## Inspection

The workshop's assessment of the vehicle before or during repair work.

## Quote

A proposed cost for labour, parts, and other charges that the customer must approve before work continues.

## Quote Approval

The customer's confirmation that the workshop may proceed with the quoted work.

## Part Request

A record of parts required for a job, including order status and expected delivery information.

## Mechanic Progress Note

An update added by a mechanic about the work being performed.

## Customer-Visible Note

A note safe to show to the customer.

## Internal Note

A note intended only for workshop staff.

## Invoice

The billing document issued to the customer after approved work is completed.

## Payment

A record of payment received against an invoice.

## Notification

A communication event intended to update the customer or workshop about an important change.

## Activity Log

A record of important actions taken in the system, including who performed the action and when.
